Production

Szaye is a leading production company in the international fashion industry.

SZAYE offers full production services of model casting, staff selection, set production, location scouting/proposal, retouching and much more to effectively create the images needed. As a steady house studio, SZAYE is your ideal and reliable partner. Our skilled and stable system makes a house photo shoot a smooth running operation. Our staff of creative photographers will create high quality visuals that coincid with our media identity. We provide the very best working environment in creating finest images. With its loft style studio in Shanghai North Bund, which is 500 square meters and of 7-meter height attached with a garden, SZAYE’s spacious studios and modern equipment give our clientele the needed edge in this rapidly changing visual market.
