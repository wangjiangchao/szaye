About Us

Szaye is a leading photography and video production company in the fashion industry.

Established in 2010, SZAYE (pronounce: /‘ðɑ:je/) is a one-stop production house in Shanghai, offering full production services, including model casting, location scouting/proposal, set production, retouching and video producing. Our line-up of top artists, ideal working environment, diversified human network, and a keen sense for globalization create the best visuals through effective communication. We consistently strive to provide our clients with a smooth and efficient communication hub in achieving desired results. To date, our team has travelled across the world – Japan, Korea, USA, and European countries… while our story is still going on… Stay tuned!
